What We Do

Texoma Classics is a full-service restoration shop located in North Texas offering worldwide service. While we specialize in the discovery and full restoration of vintage American muscle cars, one-of-a-kind rarities, and history-making classics, the vast experience of our personnel and our comprehensive set of tools give us the capacity to restore and fabricate almost anything.

How We're Helping

Texoma Classics is working with the Hurricane Harvey Relief Fund. In an effort to give back to our Texas community, we donating $1 out of every hundred from vehicle restorations in the Houston area.
